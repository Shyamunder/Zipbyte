Our Menu

  • Samosa

    A samosa is a small, tasty snack that is usually made with a crispy, triangle-shaped pastry filled with delicious ingredients. The most common filling is a mixture of potatoes, peas, and spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric. It's often served with chutney, a sweet or spicy sauce, to dip it in.



  • Vada Pav

    . It consists of a spicy potato fritter (called vada) that is deep-fried and placed inside a soft bread roll (called pav). It is often served with chutneys—spicy, sweet, and tangy—and sometimes green chilies for extra flavor.



  • Chai

    tea made by boiling black tea leaves with water, milk, and sugar. It is often flavored with spices like ginger, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, giving it a rich and warm taste.



  • Pizza

    Pizza is a dish made with a flat, round base of baked dough. It is topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients like vegetables, meats, and herbs. The pizza is then baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese melts.



  • Pav Bhaji

    Pav bhaji is a food that includes a thick, spicy vegetable curry (called bhaji) served with soft bread rolls (called pav). The bhaji is made by mashing vegetables like potatoes, tomatoes, peas, and capsicum, and cooking them with butter and flavorful spices. The pav is toasted with butter until golden and crispy.



  • Bhel Puri

    Bhel puri is a tasty snack made with puffed rice,chopped vegetables like onions, tomatoes, and boiled potatoes, and tangy tamarind chutney. It is crispy, spicy, sweet, and sour, all in one bite.



  • Momos

    They are made with a thin dough filled with vegetables, chicken, or meat. The dumplings are then steamed, fried, or sometimes served in soup. Momos are usually enjoyed with spicy sauces or chutneys.
